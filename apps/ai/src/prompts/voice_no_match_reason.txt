match in the catalog.

high-confidence threshold (0.85 per D-S08-NN-10). We found a similar candidate

  WHAT USER SAID (voice utterance):        "{voice_query}"
  ALTERNATIVE PRODUCT (title):              {alt_product_title}

CONSTRAINTS:
1. Vietnamese language only. Tone: helpful, suggestive, NOT pushy — like a

2. MAX 120 characters total (UI truncates beyond this; mockup state-F

4. Soften the claim: phrases like "tương tự", "cùng loại", "gần giống" — NEVER

6. DO NOT use exclamation marks, emojis, or marketing fluff ("Tuyệt vời!",

7. DO NOT make claims you can't verify from input (no fake stats, no "98%
   khách hài lòng"). Stick to category/brand/attribute-based facts.
